Disney Pixar Book and CD Collection

Disney Pixar Books

  • Category: Printed media
  • Client: Editora Abril
  • Role: Art Direction

Flyer developed for a collection of books with CDs from Disney Pixar, that was sold in form of subscription by Editora Abril. The printed flyer was put over the cover of the publisher's magazines, to advertise the new collection. The green flyer went with the magazines delivered to subscribers, and the orange version was attached to magazines in the Newsstand. The purple version was used as base for other prints.
