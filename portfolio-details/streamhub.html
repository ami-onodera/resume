StreamHub - Dashboard for streaming platforms

StreamHub

  • Category: Web design
  • Client: StreamHub
  • Project URL: www.streamhub.watch

StreamHub was developed by me and 3 other colleagues in 10 days, as the final project of Le Wagon's Coding Bootcamp. The website is developed using Ruby on Rails, and it's proposal is to offer an easy way to find the gaming streamers you follow on Twitch, Mixer, Youtube Gaming and other similar platforms. You can easily sign-in with 1 click using one of your accounts from streaming websites and you will automatically see in your dashboard who is online, what they are playing, number of watchers, and several other handy information.
