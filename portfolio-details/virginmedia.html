Referral portal for Virgin Media

Virgin Media referral portal

  • Category: Web design
  • Client: Virgin Media (Aklamio)
  • Project date: June, 2023
  • Project URL: https://virginmedia.aklamio.com/

While working as a webdesigner at Aklamio I had the chance to work with many big brands, like Virgin Media. This is a portal that I created for their referral program. It includes an index page, a share page, a widget, and also the "internal" pages accessible from a menu after login, that included a dashboard, a balance page, a settings page and a help page.
